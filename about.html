Our Mission

To provide a secure, stimulating, and inclusive environment where young children are empowered to explore, learn, and develop at their own pace. We strive to cultivate a lifelong love for learning through hands-on experiences.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted early childhood education center in Kumaraswamy Layout, recognized for nurturing compassionate, confident, and creative individuals equipped for future success.

A Message From The Director

"At Rainz Pre School, we don't just teach children; we partner with parents in shaping the next generation. Our commitment is to ensure that every child's first step away from home is filled with joy, safety, and endless discoveries."

Mrs. Shalini R.

Founder & Director, Rainz Pre School

Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe learning should be joyful, experiential, and child-centric.

Play-Based Learning

Children learn best when they are fully engaged in active, imaginative play.

Holistic Growth

Balancing emotional, social, physical, and cognitive development in every activity.

Emotional Security

Fostering a sense of belonging so every child feels secure to express themselves.

Our Safety Commitment

Comprehensive Safety Measures

For us, your child's safety is non-negotiable. Our campus is designed and maintained to the highest safety and hygiene standards.

  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance across all premises
  • Stringent background checks for all staff
  • Child-safe furniture with rounded edges
  • Daily multi-level sanitization routines
  • Secure pick-up verification protocols
