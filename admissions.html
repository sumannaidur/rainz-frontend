Join the Rainz Family

We are currently accepting admissions for the academic year 2026-27. Follow our simple admission process to secure a spot for your child.

1

Inquiry

Fill out the admission inquiry form on this page or call us to express your interest. Our admissions counselor will get in touch with you.

2

Campus Visit

Schedule a visit to our campus in Kumaraswamy Layout. Meet our educators, see our facilities, and understand our teaching methodology firsthand.

3

Registration & Enrollment

Complete the admission form, submit the necessary documents, and finalize the fee structure to complete the enrollment process.

Required Documents for Admission

  • Child's Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • Aadhar Card of Parents & Child (Copy)
  • 4 Passport-sized photographs of the child
  • 2 Passport-sized photographs of each parent
  • Immunization / Vaccination record (Copy)
