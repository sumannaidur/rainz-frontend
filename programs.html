Playgroup

2 - 3 Years

For many toddlers, Playgroup is their first experience away from home. We ensure this transition is gentle and comforting. Our thoughtfully crafted environment promotes active exploration, sensory play, and early social interactions.

Key Focus Areas

  • Developing gross & fine motor skills
  • Enhancing communication and vocabulary
  • Encouraging independent eating habits
  • Fostering social skills like sharing and taking turns

Nursery

3 - 4 Years

Our Nursery curriculum builds on the curiosity developed in Playgroup. We introduce structured conceptual learning, focusing heavily on hands-on activities, rhyming, storytelling, and early math concepts.

Key Focus Areas

  • Introduction to phonetics and letter sounds
  • Pre-math concepts (shapes, colors, sorting)
  • Creative expression through art and music
  • Building self-confidence and emotional intelligence

LKG (Lower Kindergarten)

4 - 5 Years

In LKG, children take significant leaps in their academic readiness. The focus shifts towards language fluency, reading readiness, and logical thinking while maintaining our core play-based methodology.

Key Focus Areas

  • Advanced phonics, blending, and early reading
  • Number concepts, counting, and simple additions
  • Environmental awareness and basic science concepts
  • Developing sustained attention spans

UKG (Upper Kindergarten)

5 - 6 Years

Our UKG program acts as a bridge to primary school. We focus heavily on independent learning, comprehensive reading, and more complex arithmetic, ensuring your child is exceptionally prepared for Grade 1.

Key Focus Areas

  • Fluent reading and basic creative writing
  • Complex math (subtraction, time, money concepts)
  • Public speaking and presentation skills
  • Project-based collaborative learning
