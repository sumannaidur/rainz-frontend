Home Facilities
A World Built for Children

Our campus in Kumaraswamy Layout is meticulously designed with safety, aesthetics, and child psychology in mind.

Smart Classrooms

Bright, well-ventilated classrooms with child-safe furniture. Equipped with age-appropriate learning manipulatives and visual aids designed to stimulate cognitive development.

Outdoor Play Area

A secure outdoor play zone featuring slides, sandpits, and open space for gross motor skill development. Rubberized flooring ensures absolute safety during active play.

CCTV Surveillance

Your child's safety is our priority. Our entire campus, including classrooms and play areas, is monitored continuously with high-definition CCTV cameras.

Activity Room

A dedicated space for art, craft, music, and dance. Helps children express their creativity freely and discover their unique hidden talents.

Mini Library

A cozy reading corner stocked with hundreds of colorful, age-appropriate picture books to inculcate reading habits right from early childhood.

Hygiene & Wellness

Strict sanitization protocols, clean and child-sized restrooms, and a dedicated sick bay for any medical emergencies to keep our little ones safe and healthy.
